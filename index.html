ABOUT US

ALIAS LUCKNOW established in 2018 is a chapter of ALIAS(AMITY LINUX ASSISTANCE SAPIENCE) which is a part of well known coding societies in the NCR. We have a mutual aim to promote the use of open source among the students and help them become great developers in the future. We believe in sharing knowledege and learning things while exploring a variety of technologies with our members specialized in versatile fields and performing well in the same with some quality projects running under them.
